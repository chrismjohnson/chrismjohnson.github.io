The Best Business in the World

08 Aug 2006

Don't wait for the best, the perfect, or the ideal. Just get moving.

Many young entrepreneurs are looking for the best way to make their money. The best way to spend their time to be productive. The best business idea to pursue. I have the holy grail of tips for these people. The search has ended. The best business in the world is...

YOURS.

If you sit around trying to perfect the best solution and the best way to do things then you will end up falling behind and never doing anything. Just pick something that you think has the possibility of success and run with it. There is always a chance it will fail, but what if it's that one in a thousand shot that doesn't!
